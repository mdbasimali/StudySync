Gulam jamun

Ingredients

  • Suger
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Milk powder: 1 cup All-purpose flour (maida): 2 tablespoons
  2. 1/8 teaspoon baking soda 2 tablespoons milk (adjust as needed for kneading)
  3. 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder Ghee or oil for deep frying
  4. 2 cups sugar 1.5 cups water 4-5 green cardamom pods (slightly crushed)

Barfi jamun

Ingredients

  • Suger
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Combine sugar and water in a pot and bring it to a boil. Add crushed cardamom pods and rose water, then simmer for 5 minutes. Set the syrup aside and keep it warm.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan over low heat. Add condensed milk and milk powder, stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  3. Drain the soaked Gulab Jamun from the syrup and place them on a plate. Take a small portion of the barfi mixture and flatten it.
  4. Wrap each Gulab Jamun with the barfi mixture, covering it completely. Garnish with chopped nuts for added flavor and decoration.
